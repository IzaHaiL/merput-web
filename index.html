About Us

Merah Putih

Welcome.

Hello, brave warriors, scattered across the vast Indonesian landscape, we extend our warmest greetings and heartfelt welcome to our illustrious server, known as Merah Putih. Merah Putih proudly stands as the preeminent haven for the Indonesian player squad community, commonly referred to as IDN. Our server was meticulously crafted with the noble purpose of forging unbreakable bonds among the valiant Indonesian squad players who grace our digital realm. Within these hallowed digital walls, we unequivocally champion the spirit of unity, embracing each and every warrior without discrimination or distinction. What's more, we are excited to share that Merah Putih boasts a wide array of divisions to cater to the diverse interests and skills of our esteemed members. So, don't hesitate to join us and become a part of our vibrant community today!

Army

Rifleman is the basic role played by most players. Riflemen are equipped with an assault rifle, pistol, grenades, and medical supplies. They are responsible for providing the main infantry firepower in combat. Support is a role that focuses on providing support for teammates. Supports are equipped with a light machine gun, smoke grenades, and medical supplies. They are responsible for providing suppressing fire, covering moving teammates, and helping to heal and revive injured teammates. Specialist is a role that focuses on special abilities. Specialists are equipped with a variety of weapons and special tools. They are responsible for carrying out special tasks, such as disarming bombs, destroying vehicles, and providing reconnaissance.

Navy

The navy is responsible for defending the country's coastline and for conducting naval operations in support of other branches of the military. The navy is also responsible for projecting power overseas and for conducting maritime security operations.

Specialist

Engineer is a role that focuses on construction and repair. Engineers are equipped with tools to build and repair structures, such as walls, barricades, and weapon stations. They can also use smoke grenades and medical supplies. Sniper is a role that focuses on long-range shooting. Snipers are equipped with a sniper rifle, pistol, grenades, and medical supplies. They are responsible for providing long-range fire support and killing enemies from a distance. Medic is a role that focuses on healing and reviving teammates. Medics are equipped with a medical bag, pistol, grenades, and medical supplies. They are responsible for healing and reviving injured teammates..
